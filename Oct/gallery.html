Meet The Team

I was born and grew up in the East Bay of San Francisco, and ended up heading to Arizona State University for college. There, I graduated with a Bachelor's in Business and Communication. I was also a walk-on, onto the Arizona State Football team. I played Wide Receiver, and was fortunate enough to see significant time on Special teams. After college, I fell into the Recruiting and Staffing industry and loved it. I worked for a large staffing firm right out of school, placing Accounting and Finance professionals. I then went to work for a Startup as employee 9, and was responsible for hiring over 200 people throughout a 5 year time period. I feel my biggest attribute is the mentality of "getting it done" at all costs. I'm a big believer in surrounding yourself with smarter people, and having no ego when trying to grow something. I'm excited to get the opportunity to build something really, really cool.
